I Started Because I Saw a Problem

Growing up in Mathur, Thanjavur, I watched local businesses struggle with their online presence. Beautiful stores. Excellent services. Loyal customers. But their websites? Slow. Outdated. Impossible to use on phones. Invisible on Google.

These businesses were losing customers — not because they weren't great at what they did, but because their websites were failing them.

That's why I became a web developer. Not to write code for the sake of it, but to help businesses like yours succeed online.

After completing my B.Sc. Computer Science at Bishop Heber College, Trichy, I didn't stop at graduation. I spent eight months in intensive certification courses — including Java certification — because I knew that theoretical knowledge wasn't enough. I needed real, practical skills to solve real business problems.

In March 2026, I joined TYM Agency as a Web Development Intern. Since then, I've worked with real clients — boutiques, artists, event planners, jewellers, hardware stores — and learned that every business has unique challenges. The one thing they share? They all need a website that actually works for them.

What Working With Me Looks Like

Hiring a web developer shouldn't feel like a black box. Here's what you can expect when we work together — transparency, collaboration, and results.

We Start With Your Goals

Before any design or code, we talk about your business. What do you need this website to achieve? More leads? Better credibility? Easier booking? Your goals become the project's North Star.

You Get Regular Updates

No disappearing developers. I share progress regularly — what's done, what's next, and any decisions that need your input. You're never in the dark about your own project.

I Ask Questions Before Making Assumptions

I don't guess what your customers want. I ask. I research. I test. Because a website built on assumptions is a website that misses its mark.

I Deliver on Deadline

Your time is valuable. I plan meticulously, communicate early about potential delays, and never promise what I can't deliver. Reliability isn't a feature — it's the baseline.

I Hand Over Clean, Documented Work

When the project is done, you get more than a website. You get clear documentation, training (if needed), and a codebase that's easy to understand and maintain. No vendor lock-in. No mystery code.

I'm Here After Launch

Websites need care. I offer maintenance packages for security updates, content changes, and performance monitoring. Or I can train your team to handle basics. Your choice, your control.
