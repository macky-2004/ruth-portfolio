Does Any of This Sound Familiar?

"Our website takes forever to load."

You know it. Your customers feel it. Slow websites kill conversions — 53% of mobile users abandon a site that takes more than 3 seconds. Google also penalizes slow sites in search rankings. If your competitors' sites load faster, they're winning your customers while you're still loading.

"Our site looks terrible on phones."

Over 60% of your customers are browsing on mobile. If your website isn't designed for thumb-scrolling, pinch-zooming, and small screens, you're losing more than half your potential business. A mobile-unfriendly site doesn't just look bad — it tells customers you don't care about their experience.

"We don't show up on Google."

You built a website, but it's invisible. Without proper SEO structure, semantic markup, and performance optimization, search engines can't understand — or rank — your site. Being on page 2 of Google is like being invisible. Your customers are searching, but they're finding your competitors instead.

"People visit but don't call or buy."

Traffic without conversion is just vanity. If visitors can't figure out what to do next — or if your contact form is buried three clicks deep — they'll leave and never return. Your website should guide visitors naturally toward the next step: calling you, booking a service, or making a purchase.

"Our site looks like it's from 2015."

First impressions matter. An outdated website makes your business look unprofessional, untrustworthy, or even closed. In a world where customers judge credibility in 0.05 seconds, your design is your handshake. Your website should reflect the quality of your work — not undermine it.

"We need a developer for every small change."

Every text update, every new photo, every price change requires calling a developer and waiting days (and paying more). Your website should empower you, not make you dependent. I build sites that are easy to maintain — or I document everything so clearly that you can make updates confidently.

If even one of these resonated with you, you're not alone. These are the most common challenges I help businesses overcome every day.

How I Turn Your Website Into a Growth Engine

I don't just "build websites." I solve business problems through thoughtful design and clean development. Every project starts with one question: What does your business need to achieve? Then I build the solution.

  1. Step 1

    Discovery & Strategy

    Before writing a single line of code, I learn about your business: Your customers. Your competitors. Your goals. Your pain points. Because a website that doesn't understand your business is just digital decoration.

    Result: A clear roadmap aligned with your actual objectives.

  2. Step 2

    User-Centered Design

    I design for the people who actually use your site — your customers. Intuitive navigation. Clear visual hierarchy. Strategic call-to-action placement. Every element guides visitors toward becoming customers.

    Result: A website that feels effortless to use and naturally converts visitors into leads.

  3. Step 3

    Lightning-Fast Development

    I write clean, semantic HTML, modern CSS, and efficient JavaScript. No bloated frameworks. No unnecessary plugins. Just optimized code that loads in under 2 seconds and ranks well on Google.

    Result: A fast, secure, low-maintenance website that search engines love and users enjoy.

  4. Step 4

    Mobile-First Approach

    I design for mobile users first, then enhance for desktop. Thumb-friendly navigation. Readable text without zooming. Fast loading on 3G networks. Because your next customer is probably browsing on their phone right now.

    Result: A seamless experience across all devices — no frustrated pinch-zooming, no broken layouts.

  5. Step 5

    Deployment & Handover

    I deploy your site to fast, reliable hosting and provide clear documentation. Need future updates? I make the process simple — or train your team to handle basic changes independently.

    Result: A website that's live, performing, and easy to maintain.
