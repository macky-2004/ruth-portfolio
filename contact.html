Questions? I've Got Answers.

What types of businesses do you work with?

I specialize in small businesses and startups across Tamil Nadu — boutiques, restaurants, service providers, creative professionals, event planners, and more. If you need a website that attracts customers and builds credibility, I can help.

How long does a typical website take?

A standard business website typically takes 2-4 weeks from our first conversation to launch. Complex projects (multi-page sites with custom features) may take 4-6 weeks. I provide a detailed timeline after understanding your specific needs.

Do you work with clients outside Thanjavur?

Absolutely! While I'm based in Mathur, Thanjavur, I work with clients across Tamil Nadu and beyond. Video calls, WhatsApp, email — modern tools make remote collaboration seamless. Many of my clients are in Trichy, Chennai, and Coimbatore.

What if I don't know what I need?

That's perfectly normal — and exactly why we start with a discovery conversation. I'll ask questions about your business, your customers, and your goals. Then I'll recommend the best approach. No technical knowledge required from your side.

Can I update the website myself after launch?

Yes. I build sites with clean, documented code and provide clear instructions for basic updates (text changes, image swaps, etc.). For more complex changes, I'm available for ongoing maintenance or can train your team.

What are your rates?

Rates depend on project scope, complexity, and timeline. I offer competitive pricing for small businesses in Tamil Nadu. After our initial consultation, I'll provide a detailed quote with no hidden costs. I also offer flexible payment plans for larger projects.

Do you offer website maintenance?

Yes. I offer monthly maintenance packages that include security updates, performance monitoring, content updates, and regular backups. Or I can handle updates on an as-needed basis. Your choice.
